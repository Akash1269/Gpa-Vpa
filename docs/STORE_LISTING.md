## Full Description (App Store & Google Play)

Keep your academic life organized with GPA Calculator — the simple, privacy-focused app for students who want to track their grades and GPA in one place.

**Key Features:**

• Instant GPA Calculation — Add your courses and see your cumulative and semester GPA update in real time on a 4.0 scale.

• Semester Organization — Courses are automatically grouped by semester. View detailed breakdowns for each term.

• Course Management — Quickly add, edit, or remove courses. Swipe to delete, undo if you change your mind.

• Academic Progress Tracking — Visual GPA chart shows your performance trend across semesters.

• Data Export — Export your records as CSV or JSON for backup or sharing.

• Beautiful Dark Mode — Switch between light and dark themes to match your preference.

• Privacy First — All data stays on your device. No account required, no data collected, no ads.

• Accessibility — Fully accessible with VoiceOver and TalkBack support.

**GPA Scale:**
A = 4.0 | A- = 3.7 | B+ = 3.3 | B = 3.0 | B- = 2.7 | C+ = 2.3 | C = 2.0 | C- = 1.7 | D+ = 1.3 | D = 1.0 | F = 0.0

Whether you're in high school, college, or graduate school, GPA Calculator helps you stay on top of your academic goals.
